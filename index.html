About Technex

TECHNEX'19, IIT BHU is one of the largest and oldest college fests in India. Embodying the true spirit of youth, Technex provides a platform for young talent from all over India to showcase their varied skills.

Technex'19 IIT BHU

With immense pleasure, we inform you that the Indian Institute of Technology, Varanasi is organizing its National Annual Techno-Management Festival, TECHNEX'19 during 8-10 MAR, 2019. We take this opportunity to extend our invitation to you to associate with TECHNEX ’19 IIT Varanasi as a Zonal Center for different Outreach Workshops. We would be greatly honored to have you as a part of it.

TECHNEX'19 is not a mere examination of Technical & Management Skills, rather it is a competition, a race of such ingenious minds put into work at this level. Sturdiness, Patience & Intellect are the skills put on display in this magnificent festival. Carving out the best in such brains infuses an all-new confidence & prepares them for hardships & battles of the future. It hones & polishes the skills of the individuals through a plethora of workshops. And some of the most influential personalities in the world motivate the assembled youth through powerful talks & their visions for the future.

We are proud of ourself organizing one of the largest festivals in India and we guarantee an experience which will leave you with new ideas, innovations, and knowledge which is going to last a lifetime!!

About Campus Ambassador Program Technex

Now, you too have a chance to become a part of this technical extravaganza. With Campus Ambassador Program you get a chance to be an extended part of the organising team of Technex’19. All you have to do is lead the contingent from your college taking part in Technex and make sure that people in your institute know about it. Be the leader in a crowd of followers.

Why Campus Ambassador?

  • An opportunity to improve your communication and managerial skills by interacting with people coming from diverse fields and from various colleges across India.
  • An opportunity to improve your leadership skills.
  • Chance to organise workshops of Technex at college level.
  • Be the face of Technex’19 in your college.

Campus Ambassador Responsibilities

  • PUBLICISE- our events in your college via different means.
  • COORDINATE- with us and help us organise workshops in your college.
  • SOCIAL MEDIA- publicize Technex in your college using the different social networking platforms.
  • TASKS- that are assigned to you must be completed before the deadline.

FAQ FAQ

  1. 1. Who can become a College Ambassador?
    ~Any College Student with a valid College Student Identity Card.
  2. 2. How to become a College Ambassador?
    ~ By Simply Registering on the College Ambassador Portal by clicking on the "Register" button at the opening page you initiate your Registration process.
  3. 3. How many CAs can be there from a college?
    ~ Can appoint more than one College Ambassador in the same college if the student participation from their college is large enough.
  4. 4. What are the conditions under which I will get a CA Certificate?
    ~ In order to get a CA Certificate, College Ambassadors will have to earn more than the minimum Cut-off points which would be set by Technex, IIT (BHU) Varanasi. The Points allocation can be seen under the tab “Points Scheme”.
  5. 5.How much do I have to work to Top the Leaderboard?
    ~ Completion of every task on time would keep you on the list of contenders for the top positions to grab the incentives of Internships and free stay at Technex'19, 2018-19.
